Mom's Chilli Recipe

Est. 1962

Ingredients

Hamburger Meat (96% lean)

1 lb

Diced Tomatoes

1 Can

White Onion

1/2 of 1

Pinto Beans

1 Can

Red Kidney Beans

1 Can

Shredded Cheese

1 Handful

Saltine Crackers

1 Handful

Seasoning

Chilli Powder

1/2 tsp.

Tony Chachere's Original Creole

1/2 tsp.

Garlic Powder

1/2 tsp.

Onion Powder

1/2 tsp.

Peprika

1/2 tsp.

Instructions

1. Cook hamburger meat on stove until all meat is brown.

2. Cook and Mix all other items (except chz and crackers) in pot.

3. Add meat to pot and mix in seasoning.

4. Let the pot sit for 45 minutes (occasionally stir).

5. Enjoy! Add cheese and crackers for extra flavor!

Serving Size = 2-4 people
